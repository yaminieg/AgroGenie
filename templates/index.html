Quick Setup Steps

  1. Complete your registration in **Profile Settings**.
  2. Go to **Location Source** and select your coordinates.
  3. Use **Field Boundary** to draw your farm boundary on the satellite workspace.
  4. Click **Finish** to compute and unlock all precision analytics dashboards.
